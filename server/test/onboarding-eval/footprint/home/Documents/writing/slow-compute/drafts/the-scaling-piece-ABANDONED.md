## the problem with this draft (note to self, later)

It's all thesis and no doubt. I sound certain and I'm not certain, and a reader can smell the gap between the confidence of the prose and the actual state of my head. The good version of this argument has the fear in it. This version pretends I don't work in ML and haven't watched the curves bend exactly the way the accelerationists said they would. Coward's draft. It hides the strongest objection because I didn't want to deal with it.
