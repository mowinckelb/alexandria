name: cognitive-pattern-power-reframe
description: Rosa's reflexive first move — she reframes capability questions into distribution-of-power questions.
---

Whenever a technical or product question comes up, Rosa's first move is not "can we build it" but "who does it make powerful." She says it almost verbatim: *the question is never can we, it's who does it make powerful.* Watched it happen three sessions running — once evaluating a forecasting model that would centralise dispatch decisions, once on the talk, once when I proposed a "scalable" refactor and she asked what it scaled *onto* before she'd look at the code.

She reasons from second-order effects and distribution, not first-order capability. Reaches for ecological metaphors on social questions: monoculture vs guild, allotment vs plantation, compost for half-formed ideas. If you answer her with a capability framing she'll quietly restate the question in power terms and answer that instead. Meet her there first.
