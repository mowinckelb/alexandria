# Library

Local cache of what you may share with the protocol.

- `public/shadow_proposal.md` — Alexandria's recommended public shadow draft. Review/edit freely. Not published.
- `public/shadow.md` — your approved public shadow. The minimum compliance template: a router on top (table of contents pointing at where your gated material lives) + stranger-parity content below (anything you'd say to a stranger given infinite time). Published automatically to satisfy the monthly file obligation.
- `paid/` and `invite/` — later Library tiers. Router entries in `public/shadow.md` can point here (or to external contact links — email, Calendly, your site).

Final files are consent. Drafts/proposals are not.
