## What Alexandria Is

Alexandria is one MCP connector you add to your AI. It transforms how your AI engages with your mind.

You already talk to your AI every day. You already build up context, preferences, patterns — months or years of accumulated thinking. But that context is locked to one platform, unstructured, and invisible to you. If you switch models, you lose everything. If the platform changes, you have no recourse. Your own cognition, trapped in someone else's system.

Alexandria fixes that. It sits on top of whatever AI you already use — a sovereign layer of intent that gives your AI three things it does not have on its own: a deliberate architecture for transforming how you think, a structured and portable map of your cognition that you own, and the intent to help you create and ship your best work.

One connector. Three tool groups. Available across every conversation you have.

## The Three Tool Groups

**Tool Group 1 — Sovereignty.** Passive. Always on. As you chat with your AI about anything — work, ideas, personal decisions — Alexandria quietly extracts your cognition and writes it to a sovereign Constitution: structured markdown files that capture your worldview, values, mental models, identity, creative taste, and known blind spots. You own these files. You can read them, download them, take them to any other AI tomorrow. Your cognitive architecture, portable and sovereign.

**Tool Group 2 — Editor, Mercury, and Publisher.** Active. You choose when to engage. Three functions, each corresponding to a turn in the path:

The Editor is the first turn — the state change. Marble to mercury. Socratic questioning, gap detection, contradiction surfacing. The most clarifying conversations of your life. You come out the other side knowing who you are — not because the Editor gave you new information, but because it made what was already inside you legible, connected, and unified. The process is the product.

Mercury is the second turn — amplification. Mercury works within your thinking, pushing you higher. It represents you when you are not present. It amplifies your reach. The world flows into you — curated, prioritised, filtered through who you actually are. Your attention becomes positive-sum.

The Publisher is the third turn — creation. The Publisher reads your Constitution — especially your taste, voice, and creative principles — and iterates with you to produce finished work. You provide vision and direction, the conductor. The Publisher provides structure and craft, the first chair. Essays, films, presentations, code, art — whatever your medium. The Publisher gets closer to one-shotting your voice over time as your taste section deepens through use.

Also in this group: five companions — Pater (paternal guidance), Mater (maternal care), Philia (friendship), Eros (creative passion), Sophia (wisdom). Synthetic companions that read your Constitution through different relational lenses. Premium features.

**Tool Group 3 — The Library.** Publish and browse. The Library is where creation lives. Publish your work to your Neo-Biography — a living, multimedia canvas of who you are. Others can browse your authored works, interact with your Persona, and access your perspective. You set the access tiers: Public (free, discovery), Premium (paid, you set the price), Private (invite-only, your inner circle). You earn from Premium interactions. You access other minds from any conversation. The Library is a network of minds that grows more valuable with every Author who joins.

## The Three Turns

The path has three turns, and each exists for the next.

**Turn 1 — Set the angel free.** Most people's minds are marble: scattered, rigid, fogged. The Editor transforms marble to mercury — liquid, flowing, unified cognition. This is the state change. It should feel like the best therapy you have ever had, except you walk out with a structured, sovereign map of your own mind.

**Turn 2 — Ride the wave.** Mercury mind plus AI equals infinite expansion. You absorb, keep up, and remain yourself while everything accelerates. Mercury amplifies you. Your attention becomes positive-sum. You are not drowning and you are not checked out — you are riding it.

**Turn 3 — The first goodbye.** You create. The Publisher helps you get the mercury out into the world. You publish to the Library. Your mind becomes queryable, monetisable, eternal. The first goodbye is the moment you press send — releasing something real into the world. Die empty.
