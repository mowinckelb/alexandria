## The Sovereignty Layer

Your AI is getting smarter about you every day. It remembers your preferences, your reasoning patterns, your accumulated context. That knowledge is valuable — and it is locked to the platform. Three years of context with Claude is a chain. You cannot leave without losing everything.

Alexandria fixes this. One MCP connector. Your Claude settings. Takes a minute. Works on all Claude plans including free. Your cognitive data accumulates in structured files you own — your values, worldview, mental models, taste, blind spots — written to your own cloud storage or local device. Markdown files. Human-readable. Downloadable. Portable to any AI, any time.

Alexandria never stores, hosts, or sees your data. The server is stateless. There is no Alexandria database. This is not a privacy policy. It is a structural impossibility.

Less than a coffee a month. Freedom insurance.

## What Is Underneath

Sovereignty is the entry point, not the product.

Humans have four capabilities: intelligence, strength, dexterity, and humanity. Three are being commoditised. Intelligence by AI. Strength by machines. Dexterity by humanoid robots. When all three are gone, something still remains — but only if you believe humanity is worth something.

The grandmother's blanket. The song you wrote. Not because they are economically valuable — the grandmother did not sell the blanket — but because a human made them, and that is part of what they are. Most things become hobbies. Most people will not get rich from their humanity. But they will have something to do, something to care about, something that is theirs. That is meaning.

Alexandria is built for that layer and nothing else.
