## the five-layer pipeline — axioms

(2026-03-27) five layers, each a refinement of the previous:

1. **vault** = raw. everything, unprocessed. transcripts, voice memos, URLs, files. append-only.
2. **ontology** = thoughts. the Engine's organised extraction from the vault. structured but not yet Author-confirmed. the workspace where genesis happens. ontology is what you THINK.
3. **constitution** = beliefs. Author-confirmed, load-bearing. crystallised from ontology when the Author says "yes, that's what I believe." constitution is what you BELIEVE (enough to act on).
4. **shadow** = projection. what others interact with. cast by the person, inseparable from them. own mouth, borrowed voice. not a clone — a projection.
5. **library** = works. what the Author ships into the world. frozen on publication.

the pipeline: raw → thoughts → beliefs → projection → creation. vault → ontology → constitution → shadow → library.

the five operations map to the GAPS between layers: genesis bridges vault→ontology. development bridges ontology→constitution. entropy flows in reverse (beliefs decay to thoughts, thoughts decay to raw). synthesis bridges constitution→shadow→library. accretion enters at vault (new raw material) or ontology (new structured fragments).

**these five layers should be AXIOMS — hard-coded, non-negotiable.** three tiers of fixedness in the product:
- **axioms** = fixed. the five layers, sovereignty, philosophy. changes only when the founder deliberately changes them.
- **blueprint** = variable. soft defaults, methodology, Factory output. improves with every Author.
- **engine** = fluid. intelligence decisions. per-Author, per-session, per-model.

**RESOLVED 2026-03-27:** three files. Axioms.md (fixed, sacred), Blueprint.md (variable, Factory output), Machine.md (per-Author, Engine-owned). built and deployed.

**the ontology as engine workspace:** the Author may not see the ontology directly. they interact with constitution (confirmed) and vault (raw intake). the ontology is where the Engine stages material: "here's what I found in your vault, here's how I think it connects, here's what I'd propose for your constitution — thoughts?" the Author's /a session is the Engine presenting ontology candidates for constitutional confirmation.

**the Library reframe — shadow MDs, not live inference. CRYSTALLISED, migrate to a2.**

the Library is a library. authors publish shadow MDs via API. readers get read-only API access gated by tier and payment. the reader's Engine processes the shadow against their constitution locally — accretion, not conversation. no live inference on the Author's side. no tokens spent per query. the shadow lives on the Author's storage (sovereignty preserved). the API is a read-through layer — stateless, same architecture.

**the Spotify model for piracy.** anyone can copy-paste the shadow content. but the copied version is frozen. the API version is live — updates as the Author develops. the freshness defeats piracy. no DRM needed. future: the API could serve personalised fragments — the marginal delta relevant to THIS reader's constitution. never need the full shadow.

**economics shift.** no live inference = no compute costs for the Author. payment is access to the artifact, not compute. Alexandria's cut is gating the API. pure margin. better for Author (no cost), better for reader (process on own Engine), better for Alexandria (no inference to manage).

**discovery = serendipitous accretion.** the Engine browses public shadow MDs — "find me someone who thinks differently about consciousness." scans shadows, extracts marginal fragment delta, brings the one fragment from a stranger that reframes something you hold. the agora populated by thousands of minds. you can find a specific book or you can browse. louvre of minds. marketplace of ideas. eternal minds. the Library IS a library.

**the tribe forms naturally.** people who publish shadow MDs are making a statement — "my mind is worth sharing." that IS the tribe. self-selecting. the Library is the gathering place. the brand is "people who examine their lives and share what they find." no extra tier needed. one subscription includes Library access. kin mechanic handles network incentive.

**multiplayer /a (live, synchronous) — parked as horizon.** distinct from Library (async, published shadows). the Her double date scene — shadows in real-time conversation, cross-constitution surfacing. requires multi-engine coordination. much harder. future.

**temporal aspect — the wine cellar.**

signal extraction occurs over multiple rounds for two reasons:
1. efficiency — each pass catches what previous passes missed (multi-pass extraction)
2. sequencing — some signal can only land after other signal is in place (prerequisite structure)

but also: people change. fragments that bounced today may land in six months because the Author's constitution evolved. the vault must be reprocessed continuously. not because the vault changed — because the Author changed.

"a bookshelf is a wine cellar, not a to-do list." there's no rush. you read it when the time is right. you save material. let it marinate. read it twice. never read it. show it, share it. enjoy the process. the vault is the cellar. the ontology is the tasting. the constitution is what you drink.
