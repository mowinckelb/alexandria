## migration thresholds — meta

when does a thought leave a0 for aN? not a fixed bar. fluid, proportional to blast radius. a thesis-level claim (bits/atoms/neurons = company roadmap) needs high confidence before entering aN because getting it wrong cascades everywhere. a product detail (editor is three roles) can enter at a lower bar because the blast radius of being wrong is small — just update it. the question is always: how much breaks if this turns out to be wrong?

## the four-layer pipeline — raw structured chosen projected

(2026-03-27) the product may have four named layers, not two:

1. **vault** = raw. everything, unprocessed. transcripts, voice memos, URLs, files. append-only.
2. **ontology** = structured. the Engine's organised extraction from the vault. thoughts, patterns, connections — structured but not yet Author-confirmed. the workspace where genesis happens. the Engine proposes, organises, connects. ontology is what you THINK.
3. **constitution** = chosen. Author-confirmed beliefs. load-bearing. the governing layer. crystallised from ontology when the Author says "yes, that's what I believe." constitution is what you BELIEVE (enough to act on).
4. **shadow** = projected. what others interact with. the external representation. cast by the person, inseparable from them. the shadow speaks with its own mouth but the Author's voice. not a clone, not a replacement — a projection.

the pipeline: raw → structured → chosen → projected. vault → ontology → constitution → shadow. each layer is a refinement. the gap between layers is where the product lives — ontology→constitution is the examined life (converting thoughts to beliefs). constitution→shadow is Turn 3 (getting it out into the world).

this mirrors the a-system: a0 (ontology/agora, raw thought) → aN (constitution/senate, crystallised) → aX (shadow/open, projected to the world).

**folder structure?** vault/ ontology/ constitution/ shadow/ works/. five folders, four layers + output. or is shadow not a folder but a function (the projection is computed from constitution, not stored separately)?

**naming question:** does the Author see these layer names? "your ontology" / "your constitution" / "your shadow" — is this user-facing vocabulary or internal product vocabulary? ontology has academic baggage but the CS meaning (structured representation of what exists in a domain) is accurate. "its all internal" as core phrase points here.

**the key product insight:** currently we go vault → constitution directly. adding ontology as an intermediate layer means the Engine has a workspace where it can organise and propose BEFORE the Author confirms. this is where the Engine does its thinking. the Author doesn't need to see the ontology — they interact with the constitution (the confirmed layer). but the ontology is where the Engine stages material for the next /a session. "here's what I found in your vault, here's how I think it connects, here's what I'd propose adding to your constitution — thoughts?"

## the atom — live augmented conversation

(2026-03-27) the minimum viable physical instantiation. constraints:

- embody the culture (attack position not person, changing mind is high-status)
- operate without the founder present
- include ai augmentation (humans at 51 can't resolve each other's haze)
- allow humans to speak verbally to each other
- /a is LIVE in the conversation, not post-processing

the atom is a PLACE, not an EVENT. a cafe, a space, an agora.

**the shadow in the room.** /a runs live. audio in, acoustics/pixels out. the shadow is IN the conversation like the Her double date — not processing afterwards. it can whisper to you (the lawyer in the deposition). it can speak on behalf of you when asked. own mouth, borrowed voice. in group chats it's easier — pixels in, pixels out. in physical conversations, the shadow listens and whispers.

**multiplayer /a.** multiple Authors' constitutions in one conversation. the ai surfaces cross-constitution contradictions and connections. "you believe X, they believe Y, and both claim Z as an axiom." this is a digital feature (bits) that can also run in the physical space (atoms).

**inner and outer agora — same architecture, different scales.** the inner agora (single /a) = dialogue between versions of you — your values vs your shadows, your 2024 self vs your 2026 self, your position on love vs your position on power. the outer agora (multiplayer /a) = dialogue between Authors — your constitution vs theirs. the architecture is identical. one scales to the other. the outer agora is still in bits — it's a digital product feature. the atom is the physical space where the outer agora also happens with bodies present.

**naming:** Priors → Agora? agora is literally what we're describing. priors was the bayesian frame (priors → posteriors). agora is the spatial frame. agora is more Alexandria-native. but priors implies posteriors which is the culture (you walk in with priors, walk out with posteriors). both work.
