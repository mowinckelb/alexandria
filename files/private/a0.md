## migration thresholds — meta

when does a thought leave a0 for aN? not a fixed bar. fluid, proportional to blast radius. a thesis-level claim (bits/atoms/neurons = company roadmap) needs high confidence before entering aN because getting it wrong cascades everywhere. a product detail (editor is three roles) can enter at a lower bar because the blast radius of being wrong is small — just update it. the question is always: how much breaks if this turns out to be wrong?

## a0 as architecture — meta

this file is itself the proof of concept. a0 is to the company what the constitution is to the Author. the company's own inner agora. the vault for company-level thinking.

**the a-system:**
- a0: marginal thought, agora, dynamic. greek thought. for ai.
- aN: ground truth, senate, codified. roman law. a1 (thesis/philosophy), a2 (product/architecture), a3 (library/brand). for ai + founder.
- aX: artifacts, open, generated. american freedom. Memo, Numbers, Logic, Concrete, Vision, abstract, etc. for humans.

naming: lowercase "a" is the brand. "ai" lowercase where the brand voice matters — philosophy docs, constitution, GTs, consumer-facing, anywhere artsy/beautiful. not a blanket rule — technical docs, investor docs, code comments can use "AI" where it reads better. "AI" is just ugly in prose. founder preference 2026-03-26, refined 2026-03-27.

**what leaves a0:** when a thought crystallises enough to operate on, it migrates to the relevant aN file. the a0 entry gets deleted (not archived — the vault has the raw capture, a0 is marginal remainder only).

**what stays in a0:** competing frames, open questions, developing ideas, parked signal, annotations on aN entries that might change.
